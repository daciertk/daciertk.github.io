About Me

Coding

I've been tinkering with computers since middle school when I received a Raspberry Pi for Christmas in sixth grade. I used it to learn python and Linux, to create robots and power electronic circuits, and some basic html websites. I went further into programming in high school at the Governor's School for Science and Technology where I learned Object Oriented Programming, Data Analysis, and physics modeling in python. I have been pursuing my degree at JMU since and plan to graduate in December 2025 with a BS in Computer Science with a Political Science minor.

Other interests and hobbies

Outside of the tech world, I love to spend my time listening to new music, playing guitar, and running. My passion though is backpacking. I love exploring new places by camping and hiking. I have hiked much of the Appalachian Trail in Virginia, and spent a month hiking the 500 mile Colorado Trail in July and August of 2024.
